***Issues with MathSciNet***

We are experiencing problems with MathSciNet with accessing some articles. Some articles are not resolving properly via the OpenAthens proxy. You might receive an error message indicating "sorry, access to this resource has been restricted." This appears to be affecting some publishers, such as AIP, Elsevier, and Springer.

We are working on the issue with OpenAthens to determine the issue. The workaround is to click on Get It @, and that should work.

Alternatively, you can connect using VPN if off-campus, and that appears to be working correctly.

(Please note that MathSciNet is working correctly while on the on-campus network).

We apologize for any inconvenience and hope to have this resolved soon.
