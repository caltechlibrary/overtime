Crossing Over: Art and Science at Caltech, 1920–⁠2020: Visit

September 27 – December 15, 2024
Wednesday–Sunday 11 am – 4 pm
Closed Mondays and Tuesdays
Closed November 28 and 29

Free admission

Located on Caltech’s campus, the exhibition begins at Bechtel Mall on S. Wilson Ave., just north of E. California Blvd. It continues in Linde Laboratory, Gates Annex, Dabney Hall, and the Chen Building. A map is coming soon on this site.

Self-serve paid visitor parking is available in the South Wilson Avenue parking structures, south of E. Del Mar Blvd.:

Parking Structure 1: 341 S. Wilson Ave.
Parking Structure 2: 405 S. Wilson Ave.

The pay station for both structures is located in Parking Structure 1.

Some restricted parking is available on adjacent streets.

We are 1.2 miles from the Metro A Line train, which connects Pasadena to Los Angeles Union Station and several other stations in Los Angeles, Long Beach, and the San Gabriel Valley. The closest stations for walking are Del Mar to our west and Lake to our north. Caltech is connected to the A Line and other points in Southern California by several bus lines, including the Metro 177 and 267 buses and the Pasadena 10 bus.
