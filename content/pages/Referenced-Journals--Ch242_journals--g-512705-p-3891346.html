Frequently Referenced Journals

Several journals are referenced frequently in Ch 242. Most you can find using PubFinder . Use the publisher link when available. LInks from PubFinder will be proxied using the OpenAthens authentication service. You will be prompted for your access.caltech username and password.

The journals listed below may be a little more tricky to find due to publisher or title changes. Detailed on access for these titles are listed.

If you have any trouble with access, please contact me.
