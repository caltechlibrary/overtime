Domestic Violence Awareness Month (DVAM) is a national campaign dedicated to raising awareness about domestic violence. Learn more at The Domestic Violence Awareness Project (DVAP) .

On Campus Advocate

Pilar Montenegro is an employee with Peace Over Violence, who has partnered with Caltech's Equity and Title IX Office , to offer free services to undergraduate and graduate students, staff and faculty. Pilar is one of your confidential resources, who is available for private conversations. She is also a trained professional who is here to listen and help you find options that are appropriate and specific to your needs. Pilar can answer any questions about the reporting process, on campus or with police, explain survivor rights and address any physical or emotional safety concerns you might be experiencing. She is here as your confidential source for emotional support, validation, and advocacy while placing your needs first.
