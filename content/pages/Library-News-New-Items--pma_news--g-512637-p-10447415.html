Keenious Research Assistant

The Caltech Library has subscribed to a new research tool called Keenious . Keenious makes recommendations for further reading based either on an existing text or PDF documents using a variety of techniques, including a language model to incorporate semantics of the texts and topics. Keenious is a very different type of search tool than you might be used to. With Keenious, you use your current document as an input into the search, compared to using just keywords as done in traditional search engines. Access the Keenious Help Center .
