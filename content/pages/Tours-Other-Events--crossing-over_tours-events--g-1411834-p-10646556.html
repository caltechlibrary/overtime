November 16, 2024 – Jane Brucker Exhibition & Discussion

For the Crossing Over exhibition , artist Jane Brucker created six softly lit installations that sit within Gates Annex Library’s bookshelves. Her installation creates a dialogue with Caltech’s collection of rare, illustrated books in physics and astronomy. On Saturday, November 16 from 11 am–1 pm, Brucker herself will be present at Gates to discuss her work.

December 6, 2024 – PST ART: Caltech History & Architectural Tour

Docents will meet attendees opposite 525 S. Wilson Avenue, on the northeast corner of S. Wilson Avenue and E. California Blvd, for a free 90 minute architectural tour of Caltech's beautiful campus along with viewing the Crossing Over: Art and Science at Caltech, 1920-2020 exhibit. Advance RSVP required . Tour attendance is limited. Please arrive early to allow time for finding parking at Caltech . Tour will leave promptly at 11:00 a.m.
