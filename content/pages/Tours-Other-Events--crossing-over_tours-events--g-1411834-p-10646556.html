November 7 and December 6: Caltech History & Architectural Tours

Docents will meet attendees opposite 525 S. Wilson Avenue, on the northeast corner of S. Wilson Avenue and E. California Blvd, for a free 90 minute architectural tour of Caltech's beautiful campus along with viewing the Crossing Over: Art and Science at Caltech, 1920–2020 exhibit. Advance RSVP required . Tour attendance is limited. Please arrive early to allow time for finding parking at Caltech. Tour will leave promptly at 11:00 a.m.

November 7, 13, 15, and 20: Undergraduate Student Tours

Join exhibition organizers for a tour for Caltech undergraduate students.

November 16: Jane Brucker Visit

Artist Jane Brucker created six softly lit installations that sit within Gates Annex Library’s bookshelves. Her installation creates a dialogue with Caltech’s collection of rare, illustrated books in physics and astronomy. On Saturday, November 16 from 11 am to 1 pm, Brucker herself will be present at Gates to discuss her work.
