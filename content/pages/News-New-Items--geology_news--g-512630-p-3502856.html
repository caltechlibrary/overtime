GeoRef - Engineering Village - two-month trial

The Library has a 2-month trial through May 31 to GeoRef via Elsevier’s Engineering Village platform. GeoRef is a database established by the American Geoscienes Institute (AGI) and contains over 4 million references to geoscience journal articles, books, maps, conference papers, reports, and theses. The database covers the geology of North America from 1666 to the present, and world coverage from 1933 to the present.

Currently, the Library has access to GeoRef via EBSCO’s platform .

We are actively trying a new platform via Elsevier to see if it better meets the needs of our researchers. To access GeoRef via Elsevier’s Engineering Village , please visit this link . (NOTE: Be sure and un-check Inspec, as Inspec is not part of the trial).
