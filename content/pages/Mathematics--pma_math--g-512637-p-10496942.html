American Institute of Mathematics (AIMS)

AIMS is a mathematical sciences research institute supported by the NSF.

They have a collection of materials housed in Caltech Hall 9th floor, and their offices are on the 8th floor of Caltech Hall.

Their collection of materials can be found in their catalog .

Please note that this collection is not part of the Caltech Library catalog or collection . Please contact AIMS for more information: https://aimath.org/about/contact/
