Copyright Tip

Many publishers, particularly in the US, use third party company Copyright Clearance Center (CCC) as a clearing house for permissions and reuse requests for content in copyrighted publications. Graduate students often use this service when including published content as part of their theses, for example. Faculty and students have often used the service when wanting to reuse a full or partial published image from a journal article or a book. The user receives an online document to show they have the requisite permissions.

Technical Report Archive & Image Library (TRAIL)

Caltech is a member of the Technical Report Archive & Image Library (TRAIL) , a grass-roots open access organization that identifies, acquires, catalogs, digitizes, and offers unrestricted access to U.S. government agency technical reports, while providing top tier metadata to improve discoverability. The mission of TRAIL is to ensure preservation, discoverability, and persistent open access to government technical publications regardless of form or format.
