Open Access Policy Waiver Request
Some publishers require a waiver form exempting a Caltech author from the Caltech Open Access policy when publishing in one of their journals. Most commonly this happens with publications in Science , Nature , and PNAS . This form is only required for Caltech professorial and research faculty authors. The Caltech Open Access Policy applies exclusively to tenured/tenure-track faculty, research faculty, and postdoctoral scholars with appointments on the Caltech campus . The policy does not apply to research scientists, other staff, or graduate and undergraduate students.

Link to waiver form

If you have any questions about the Open Access Policy please contact us at coda@library.caltech.edu .

For more information about Caltech Library support for Open Access please visit our Open Access Publishing Support page.

TechHub Poster & Map Printing

The TechHub has acquired a large format printer that is now available to print posters and maps with a maximum printable width of 35.6 inches. Large format prints are charged at $8/linear foot. To avoid pixelation when printing, please provide high resolution images (ideally between 150 and 300 DPI). Large format printing services are open to Caltech students, staff, faculty, and postdocs.

Lost Fee Policy

Starting August 31st, if a patron checks out an item and does not renew or return the item within 30 days after it becomes overdue, that item will become flagged as “lost” in FOLIO, and the patron’s library account will be blocked and assessed a fee. If you still have the item, you can return it and immediately have the fee removed and your account reactivated. Please contact us at library@caltech.edu with any questions.

LabArchives
Electronic laboratory notebooks (ELNs) are used by many researchers to document experiments, data, protocols, and collaborations within teams. Caltech has a site license with LabArchives to provide the campus community with access to ELNs and unlimited use of this platform. LabArchives offers short training sessions at their website , where you can also log in by choosing Caltech as your institution and then enter your access.caltech credentials.

Publons

Publons is a service offered by the Caltech Library that will allow researchers to more easily demonstrate the impact of their work. Publons is used globally to track publications, citations, peer reviews, and journal affiliations in one place. All publications are easily imported from the Web of Science, ORCID, or your bibliographic reference manager (e.g. EndNote or Mendeley). Publons provides trusted citation metrics, automatically imported from the Web of Science Core Collection. Researchers can access a verified peer review and journal editing history, powered by partnerships with thousands of scholarly journals. They can also download a record summarizing their scholarly impact as an author, editor, and peer reviewer.

Libby App
The Caltech Library offers access to Libby , the library reading app from Overdrive that lets users borrow ebooks and audiobooks for free. We are currently in the process of adding more items to our catalog, so feel free to get in touch to recommend selections. To access Libby, go to your app store of choice, download and open the app, and then choose "I have a library card," search for Caltech in the list, and log in using your Access credentials. If you save an item to your wishlist, you'll be notified when it's added to the Library catalog. Recent updates make Libby more accessible for all readers, including users with a diverse range of visual, motor, and cognitive needs.
