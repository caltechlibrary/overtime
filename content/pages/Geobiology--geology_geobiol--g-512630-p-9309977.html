Paleobiology Database

The Paleobiology Database is a public database of paleontological data that anyone can use, maintained by an international non-governmental group of paleontologists.

You can use the navigator map to search for data. You can filter fossil occurrences by time, space, and taxonomy, and show the displays in their modern and paleogeographic locations.
